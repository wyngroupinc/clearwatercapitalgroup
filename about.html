About Clearwater Capital Group

A modern financing firm for people who want to understand the decision

We built Clearwater around a simple belief: financing should feel clear, modern, and advisory — closer to the software you use every day than the paperwork you used to dread.

Advisory-first, not transaction-first

Plenty of companies can process a loan. Our focus is helping you make a sound decision — understanding your numbers, weighing the trade-offs, and choosing a structure that fits where you're trying to go.

From a first-time buyer's pre-approval to a seasoned investor's cash-out refinance, we bring the same discipline: clarity, speed, and a strategy that's actually yours.
